Loose Ends Privacy Policy

AI Brain Dump Sorter

Effective July 17, 2026

The short version

Loose Ends has no account, no ads, no trackers, and no analytics SDKs. Everything you capture is stored only on your device. We collect as little as technically possible to make the app work.

What stays on your device

Every thought you capture — its text, the bucket it's filed in, any extracted date/item/person, and your honesty check-in answers — is stored only in this app's local storage on your device. We cannot see it, there is no server-side account, and deleting the app deletes it.

Voice capture

If you speak instead of type, Loose Ends transcribes your voice to text entirely on-device using Apple's on-device speech recognition where available. Your audio is never uploaded or stored by us — only the resulting text is ever used, and only for the app's own filing and check-in features.

What leaves your device

If you use Pro's AI auto-triage or honesty check-ins, the raw text of the specific thought you captured (plus, for check-ins, whatever date/item/person was already extracted from it) is sent over an encrypted connection to our AI processing service so it can classify the thought or write the check-in question. We do not send your name, email, or any account identifier — Loose Ends has none to send. That request is processed to generate the answer and is not stored by us afterward.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. Apple provides us anonymous receipts to unlock Pro features.

Children

Loose Ends is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.
