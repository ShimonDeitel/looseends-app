How it works

No filing. No thinking about it.

The mental tax was never the task — it was deciding what kind of thing it is and where it goes. Loose Ends does that part for you.

Say it or type it

One box. No categories to pick, no forms to fill. Speak it out loud or type it in three seconds flat.

AI sorts it automatically

Reminder, calendar event, shopping item, or follow-up — classified instantly, with the date, item, or person pulled out if it's there.

It checks back

Later, when the moment's passed, Loose Ends asks one direct question: did you actually do it? No hiding from the answer.

The quirky part

An honesty streak, not a gold star

7
current streak

"Did you call the dentist?"

The next time you open the app after a check-in period passes, Loose Ends asks about one specific thing you captured — by name. Answer honestly and it tracks a plain streak of loops actually closed versus ones you let quietly die. No badges, no confetti, no fake rewards. Just a number that tells you the truth.
